Chocolate Chip Cookies

These homemade cookies are soft, chewy, and full of chocolate flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Beat butter and sugar, then add the egg.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients and fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until golden brown.
